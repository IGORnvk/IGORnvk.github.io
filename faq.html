Frequently Asked Questions

How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

You should visit HZ print website to do it. Firstly, log in, choose file(s) that you want to print and confirm the request. Than, you can go to HZ building, find the printer, scan your student card and print the documents.

How can you scan a document a send it to your laptop at HZ?

All the information you need about that can be found on this page

How can I buy something (e.g when I sign up for the IT introduction event on 16/09) on the HZ web shop?

You should go to HZ Webshop and log in to your account. That's all - Congratulations! You now can purchase whatever you want.

Where can I find the latest instructions on what to do when you are sick / show symptoms of COVID?

You should visit HZ coronavirus FAQ for detailed information.

How can you book a project space in one of the wings?

At first, you should go to SelService Portal. After that, choose "Make a reservation" → "Reserve meeting room or Project Group room" → "Reserve a room".

What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

Free parking is possible on the car park of PZEM at the Poelendaelesingel 10 in Middelburg. This is a few minutes' walk to the HZ. Unfortunately, it is not possible to park directly at the HZ building. However, there are two disabled parking spaces and spaces for HZ cars near the building itself.
